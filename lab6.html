Characters

The characters shown below are seen in the Marvel Universe excluding two.

Gwenpool

She is from another universe but somehow ended up in the Marvel universe. She doesn't have any superpowers, only the knowledge of the heroes, villains, and their abilities. She learned how to use from her mentor, Batroc.

War Machine

He is a former Air Force Lieutenant by the name of James "Rhodey" Rhodes. The longtime best friend of Tony Stark. His armor is equipped with a variety of projectile weapons.

Emma Frost

She is a villain of the Hellfire Club but turned into one of the X-Men. She has telepathic abilities. Also has manipulation skills.

Mysterio

He is the Master of Illusions. He is also part of the Sinister 6. His plans consist of taking down his number one enemy: Spider-man.

Shuri

She is the Princess of Wakanda. She became Black Panther after her brother, T'Challa, passed away. She has no spuer human abilities.

Dr. Doom

He has become one of Marvel's greatest villains. He planned to reshape the planet into his image, and if he failed, he would destroy it. He placed a hot iron mask onto his face and let it cool, permanently binding it to his face.

Peelverine

He is Fortnite's variant of Wolverine. He is a banana with Wolverine's blue and yellow suite.

Captain Jones

He is Fortnite's variant of Captain America. He wears Captain America's suit with a blue and silver shield.
